# YouTube Ads Plan: Times Table Animals

## Campaign Type
Video campaign — Skippable In-Stream + YouTube Search

## Objective
Awareness + First Visit (drive parents and teachers to try the game)

## Audience Targeting

### In-Market Audiences
- Educational games for kids
- Elementary / primary school parents
- Children's learning apps

### Custom Intent (keyword-based)
- times table animals
- times tables game kids
- multiplication game children
- fun math practice kids
- learn times tables game

### Demographic
- Age: 25-54 (parent/teacher decision-makers)
- Devices: All

### Placement Exclusions
- Gaming (violent/mature)
- Music videos unrelated to kids/education
- Mature content

---

## Ad Formats

### 1) Skippable In-Stream (most reach)
Plays before other videos. User can skip after 5 seconds.
You are charged only when user watches 30s or more.

**Hook (0-5s — must work before skip)**
> "Your child dreads times tables? This changes everything."

**Body (5-30s)**
> "Times Table Animals is a free online game where kids practice multiplication through fun animal habitats.
> They help Bunny in the Meadow, solve with the Dragon, and explore 12+ incredible worlds.
> No app download. No signup. Just open and play."

**CTA (last 5s)**
> "Search: Times Table Animals — and start the adventure today."

---

### 2) Bumper Ad (6 seconds, non-skippable)
Short awareness burst. Used to reinforce brand recall.

**Script**
> "Kids learning times tables? Try Times Table Animals — search it today."

---

### 3) YouTube Search Ad (In-Feed Video)
Appears in YouTube search results when parents search for related terms.

**Title**
Times Table Animals | Fun Multiplication Game for Kids

**Description line 1**
Practice times tables through animal habitats and game rewards.

**Description line 2**
Loved by kids, trusted by parents and teachers.

---

## Video Production Notes

### Suggested Shots
1. Child looking bored/frustrated with flashcards (relatable pain point)
2. Screen recording of game loading — habitat selection screen
3. Quick gameplay: multiplication question, correct answer, celebration
4. Progress/rewards screen showing new habitat unlocked
5. Happy child reacting to progress
6. Final frame: "Times Table Animals" title on screen with URL

### Tone
- Warm, encouraging, playful
- Speak to parents, not kids (they control clicks and budget)
- Avoid edgy humor; keep it trust-building

### Video Length Recommendations
- In-Stream: 30-60 seconds
- Bumper: exactly 6 seconds
- In-Feed: 60-90 seconds (more demonstration)

## Budget Suggestion
- In-Stream: $15/day
- Bumper: $5/day
- In-Feed: $10/day
- Test period: 14 days

---

## KPI Targets
- View rate (In-Stream): > 30%
- Click-through rate: > 1%
- Cost per view: < $0.05 (adjust by market)
- Site visits from YouTube trending upward week-over-week

---

## Optimization Actions (Weekly)
1. Pause placements (channels/videos) with high impressions and zero clicks.
2. Test 2 different hooks weekly.
3. Compare bumper vs in-stream CTR — scale the winner.
4. Retarget viewers of video ads with a direct CTA in-stream ad.
